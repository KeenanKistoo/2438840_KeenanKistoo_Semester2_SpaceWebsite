Solar Flare Game (Data-Driven Artwork):

In this game, you will be in space for 7 days (not literally, it'll probably be about 2 mins). You will need to monitor and maintain your technological devices to ensure that you will safely return home.

In this time-based quiz game, you will need to notify the astronauts, air traffic control AND/OR telecommunications if:

They have maintenance, flights, or other tasks scheduled at the same time as a solar flare burst.

REMEBER: SOLAR FLARE BURSTS START AND END AT A SPECIFIC TIME. ANYTIME IN BETWEEN THEN WILL AFFECT THE ASTRONAUTS, AIR TRAFFIC CONTROL, AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS

Finally, look at the first letter of the source location and select either, northen hemisphere or southern hemisphere.

GOOD LUCK!
