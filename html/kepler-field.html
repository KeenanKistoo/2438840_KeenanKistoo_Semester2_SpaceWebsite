What are Solar Flares?

Solar flares are sudden and intense bursts of energy and radiation emanating from the Sun's surface and atmosphere. These eruptions are caused by the release of magnetic energy stored in the Sun's atmosphere, particularly in regions known as sunspots, which are areas of intense magnetic activity.

The TIME FORMAT is in ISO 8601 format. If we look at the first element marked ACTIVE REGION NO.: 13359, we can understand the time format as:

2023-07-01 - Date

'T' - Separates the date from the time

'Z' - "Zulu Time" or more specifically, Coordinated Universal Time (UTC)
