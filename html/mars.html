What are Asteroids NeoWs?

Asteroids NeoWs (Near-Earth Object Web Service) is a NASA (National Aeronautics and Space Administration) web service that provides data and information about Near-Earth Objects (NEOs). NEOs are a group of celestial objects that include asteroids and comets that have orbits that come into close proximity to Earth. NEOs are of particular interest to scientists and astronomers because of the potential impact hazard they pose to our planet.
